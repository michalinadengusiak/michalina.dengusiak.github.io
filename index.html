ABOUT ME

I love programming!

Hi! I am Michalina, I am now currently 11 years old and go to grade 7. In my short life, I have already accomplished quite a sum of things. My father is a computational engineer so I was exposed to computers at a very young age. First of all, when I was 4 me and my father created a post about how the Honeybee component isn't yellow. We designed all the colors that bees could have and we did showed it to Mostapha. Then, I explored Revit and designed my own house there with furniture and trees. It was amazing! I always wanted to work on it non-stop!!! When I was 5 years old, an architect did a workshop about designing houses and we were supposed to make our own one out of cardboard. I did what he wanted and made my own house. As I went home and my father noticed that I did that he said we could do a simulation on it in Grasshopper. I had no idea what he was talking about but agreed anyways. So he opened up Grasshopper and together we did a sunoath and put my model in Rhino and looked at the different climates. I found this very interesting and it was a lot of fun. Then since I had a humongous amount of fun my father thought that maybe we should try programming. That's how I started out with Scratch a program that seemed like a game to me. I would spend a lot of time their working on new games, and without my knowledge getting a head start into the computational world. Then, I found out about Python, a basic yet fun program. It grabbed my attention immediately since it was easy yet it gave you a lot of possiblities to what you wanted to do with it. So,I did a course on Sololearn to deepen my knowledge about it. That was a huge accomplishment since now I can do some things on Python. Afterwards, my father was working on Ladybug Tools Spider gbXML Viewer, and I helped with recording the videos to that. I was surprised and very pleased to be invited to PARAMETRIA . It was an amazing experience which gave me the opportunity to see what can be accomplished with Rhino. It was led by Mateusz Zwierzycki and we were creating a Responsive Facade that would in summer block the sunlight whilst in winter take in the sunlight. We programmed it first with Grasshopper components and then put it into C# code. I was surprised to see that you could do so much with Rhino!
